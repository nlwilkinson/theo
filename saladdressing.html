Mushroom Pasta

Ingredients

  • Chestnut mushrooms
  • Pasta
  • Red onion
  • Parsley
  • White wine
  • Garlic
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Boil ketttle and cook pasta per packet instructions
  2. Whilst pasta is cooking, fry the onions, garlic, and mushrooms in separate pan until soft
  3. Add wine to pan ansd stir until it cooks off
  4. Season
  5. Top with parsley just before serving
