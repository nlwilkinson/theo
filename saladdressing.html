Best Ever Salad Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1 x garlic clove (crushed)
  • 1 x tsp dijor or grainy mustard
  • Salt and pepper
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Balsamic Vingear
  • 1 x tsp sugar
  • 2 x tsp natural plain yoghurt

Method

  1. Place crushed garlic clove in bottom of empty jam jar
  2. Add mustard
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  4. Add sugar
  5. Add approx half and half vinegar and oil
  6. Whisk thoroughly
  7. Add yoghurt
  8. Whisk thoroughly once more
