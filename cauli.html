Cauliflower Cheese

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and black pepper

Method

  1. Mix the flour and butter together in a pan and whisk until it forms a roux
  2. Slowly add milk and continue to whisk so the mixture binds
  3. Season
  4. Add plenty of grated cheddar cheese
  5. Cut up cauliflower and boil for around 5-6 mins
  6. Place everything together in one dish
  7. Bake for around 20 mins until golden on top
