Home Made Mayo

Ingredients

  • 1 x egg yolk
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 x tsp dijon mustard
  • Vegetable/sunflower/groundnut oil (not olive oil)
  • Drizzle of white wine vinegar
  • Half lemon (juice)

Method

  1. Whisk egg yolk, salt and pepper, and mustard in a glass bowl
  2. Slowly drizzle in the oil bit by bit until the mixture thickens
  3. Drizzle in some white wine vinegar to thin out
  4. Whisk
  5. Add more oil (3 or 4 glugs); whisk inbetween adding glugs until thickens again
  6. Squeeze in 1/2 lemon's quice and stir
  7. Season to taste
